# Energy and Utilities Cloud

The Energy & Utilities Cloud extends Salesforce Customer 360 to provide solutions for the energy and utilities industry. It helps businesses establish their presence online, improve operational efficiency, and deliver industry-standard customer experiences. For more information refer [Energy & Utilities Cloud](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.energy_energyutilities_cloud_258980.htm&type=5&language=en_US)

-   **[Energy and Utilities Cloud Standard Objects](atlas.en-us.eu_developer_guide.meta/eu_developer_guide/eu_objects_overview.htm)**
    Use standard objects to work with the E&U Cloud.
-   **[E&U Cloud Resources](atlas.en-us.eu_developer_guide.meta/eu_developer_guide/connect_resources_eu_resources.htm)**
    E&U Cloud Business APIs are RESTful APIs.
