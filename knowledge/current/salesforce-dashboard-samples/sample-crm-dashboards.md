# Sample CRM Dashboards

You can download sample dashboards from AppExchange.

Go to [https://appexchange.salesforce.com/home](https://appexchange.salesforce.com/home "HTML (New Window)") and search for “Salesforce CRM Dashboards” to download and install the package containing the following dashboards and underlying reports. (Your dashboards may appear different, depending on your data. Screenshots in this guide may differ slightly from the ones in the package.)

-   [Marketing Executive Dashboard](atlas.en-us.salesforce_dashboard_samples.meta/salesforce_dashboard_samples/salesforce_sample_dashboards_sales_and_marketing_MED.htm)
-   [Sales Executive Dashboard](atlas.en-us.salesforce_dashboard_samples.meta/salesforce_dashboard_samples/salesforce_sample_dashboards_sales_and_marketing_SED.htm)

-   [Salesperson Dashboard](atlas.en-us.salesforce_dashboard_samples.meta/salesforce_dashboard_samples/salesforce_sample_dashboards_sales_and_marketing_SPD.htm)
