# Feed Items Resources

Get, search, delete, bookmark, and like feed items. Get and post comments. Get and vote in polls. Get, add, and remove topics. In Experience Cloud sites, get a flagged post, flag a post for moderation, and delete moderation flags.

![Important](/docs/resources/img/en-us/260.0?doc_id=images%2Ficon_note_important.png&folder=chatterapi)

#### Important

Feed Item Resources aren’t available in version 32.0 and later. In version 32.0 and later, use [Feed Elements Resources](atlas.en-us.chatterapi.meta/chatterapi/connect_resources_feed_elements.htm "Information about feed elements. Access, edit, post, search, and delete feed elements. Access a feed element’s capabilities, including bundles and their feed elements.").

-   **[Feed-Items Batch](atlas.en-us.chatterapi.meta/chatterapi/conenct_resources_feed-items_batch.htm)**
    Get up to 500 feed items in one request
-   **[Feed-Items Query](atlas.en-us.chatterapi.meta/chatterapi/connect_resources_feed-items_FeedItemsQuery.htm)**
    Search for feed items.
-   **[Feed-Items, Specific Feed Item](atlas.en-us.chatterapi.meta/chatterapi/connect_resources_feed-items_SpecificFeedItem.htm)**
    Get, delete, or bookmark a feed item.
-   **[Feed Items Comments](atlas.en-us.chatterapi.meta/chatterapi/connect_resources_feed-items_FeedItemComments.htm)**
    Get comments for a feed item or post a comment.
-   **[Feed-Items Likes](atlas.en-us.chatterapi.meta/chatterapi/connect_resources_feed-items_FeedItemLikes.htm)**
    Get likes for a feed item or like a feed item.
-   **[Feed-Items Polls](atlas.en-us.chatterapi.meta/chatterapi/connect_resources_feed-items_FeedPolls.htm)**
    Get a poll or a vote on a poll.
-   **[Feed-Items Topics](atlas.en-us.chatterapi.meta/chatterapi/connect_resources_feed-items_FeedItemTopics.htm)**
    Add a topic to a feed item. Remove a topic from a feed item.
-   **[Moderation Flags on a Feed Item](atlas.en-us.chatterapi.meta/chatterapi/connect_resources_communities_moderation_feed_item.htm)**
    In Experience Cloud sites, get a flagged post, flag a post for moderation, and delete moderation flags.
