# Retired Resources

These resources are retired.

-   **[Commerce Einstein Resources](atlas.en-us.chatterapi.meta/chatterapi/connect_resources_commerce_einstein_resources.htm)**
    Get the status of a Commerce Einstein deployment. Get the Commerce Einstein configuration. Export and purge Commerce Einstein activity.
-   **[Commerce Webstore Promotions, Associate Action](atlas.en-us.chatterapi.meta/chatterapi/connect_resources_commerce_webstore_promotions_actions_associate.htm)**
    Gets the associated promotions and price adjustments for a cart and its items. Only the 50 highest priority eligible promotions are processed.
-   **[Commerce Webstore Promotions, Execute Action](atlas.en-us.chatterapi.meta/chatterapi/connect_resources_commerce_webstore_promotions_actions_execute.htm)**
    Applies associated promotion discounts to a cart and its items.
-   **[Datacloud Resources](atlas.en-us.chatterapi.meta/chatterapi/connect_resources_datacloud_resources.htm)**
    Information about Datacloud resources used to purchase Data.com contacts or companies. Use the Datacloud resources to purchase Data.com contacts or companies and get information about your purchases, including how many credits are available for your use. You can also add contacts to selected companies or import contacts to your CRM.
-   **[Feed Items Resources](atlas.en-us.chatterapi.meta/chatterapi/connect_resources_feed_items.htm)**
    Get, search, delete, bookmark, and like feed items. Get and post comments. Get and vote in polls. Get, add, and remove topics. In Experience Cloud sites, get a flagged post, flag a post for moderation, and delete moderation flags.
-   **[Feeds Resources](atlas.en-us.chatterapi.meta/chatterapi/connect_resource_feeds_feed_items.htm)**
    Get feed items for feeds. Post feed items to feeds.
-   **[Forecasting Resources](atlas.en-us.chatterapi.meta/chatterapi/connect_resources_forecasting_retired.htm)**
    Get jump-to information, managers in a hierarchy, and user access to forecasts.
-   **[Social Engagement Resources](atlas.en-us.chatterapi.meta/chatterapi/connect_resources_social_engagement_list.htm)**
    Provide social functionality, such as access to managed social accounts and social post intents, for service agents.
-   **[Social Engagement Setup Resources](atlas.en-us.chatterapi.meta/chatterapi/connect_resources_social_engagement_setup_list.htm)**
    Configure the Social Customer Service product.
-   **[User Resources](atlas.en-us.chatterapi.meta/chatterapi/connect_resources_users_retired.htm)**
    Change the status of a conversation. Get, post, and crop a user photo. Get the most recent user status feed item. Update or delete user status.
-   **[Zones Resources](atlas.en-us.chatterapi.meta/chatterapi/connect_resources_zones.htm)**
    Information about Chatter Answers zones.
