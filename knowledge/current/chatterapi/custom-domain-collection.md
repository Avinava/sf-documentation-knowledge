# Custom Domain Collection

List of custom domains for the org. A custom domain is a domain that you own, such as https://www.example.com, that serves content from your Experience Cloud sites or Salesforce Sites.

| Property Name | Type | Description | Filter Group and Version | Available Version |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| domains | Custom Domain[] | List of custom domains.If no custom domains exist in the org, an empty list is returned. | Small, 60.0 | 60.0 |
