# Assign Access to AppExchange Marketplace Analytics

To give your team access to AppExchange Marketplace Analytics, assign the Manage Listings permission in the Salesforce Partner Community.


| User Permissions Needed |
| --- |
| To assign permissions to Partner Community users: | Manage Users |

![Note](/docs/resources/img/en-us/260.0?doc_id=images%2Ficon_note.png&folder=packagingGuide)

#### Note

The Manage Listings permission provides access to all Partner Console features, including the ability to create, edit, and publish listings.

1.  Log in to the [Salesforce Partner Community](https://partners.salesforce.com/).
2.  Click **Manage Users**.
3.  Search for a user at your company.
4.  Under Listings, select the checkbox.
