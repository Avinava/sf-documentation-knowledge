# Access the AppExchange Partner Console

The Partner Console is the complete business management site for ISV partners and consultants with AppExchange listings. It’s where partners create listings, manage security reviews, and monitor solution performance metrics.

To access the Partner Console you must have a Salesforce Partner Community login. Learn more in the [Partner Community Registration Guide](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=000390506&type=1&language=en_US "HTML (New Window)").


| User Permissions Needed |
| --- |
| To access the AppExchange Partner Console: | Manage Listings |

1.  Log in to the [Salesforce Partner Community](https://partners.salesforce.com/ "HTML (New Window)").
2.  Click **Publishing** to launch the Partner Console.
