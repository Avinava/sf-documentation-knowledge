# Why can't my customer make an AppExchange Checkout purchase?

> If a customer clicks Get It Now on your listing, but can't make a
    Checkout purchase, verify that the customer is logged in to AppExchange with a supported
    Salesforce org. Checkout supports only paid orgs whose status is Active.
    Trial orgs, sandbox orgs, and Developer Edition orgs aren't supported.
