# View Checkout Management App Logs

The Checkout Management App (CMA) creates logs when connecting to Stripe or syncing your data. If you experience issues with the CMA, view logs to help diagnose their cause.


| User Permissions Needed |
| --- |
| To manage apps: | Customize Application |
| To view CMA logs: | CMA Admin User |

1.  Log in to the org where the CMA is installed.
2.  To view CMA logs in Lightning Experience:
    1.  Open the App Launcher, and click **Other Items**.
    2.  Click **Checkout Logs**.
3.  To view CMA logs in Salesforce Classic:
    1.  Open the App Launcher, and click **Checkout Management App**.
    2.  Click the plus icon (+) next to the main tabs.

        ![A view of the plus icon used to open additional tabs in the Checkout Management App](/docs/resources/img/en-us/260.0?doc_id=appexchange%2Fcma%2Fimages%2Fcma_tabs.png&folder=packagingGuide)

    3.  Click **Checkout Logs**.
