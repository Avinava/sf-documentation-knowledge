-   **[isSuccess](atlas.en-us.packagingGuide.meta/packagingGuide/apex_COA_ServiceOrderCloneOutput_isSuccess.htm)**
    Indicates the success of the clone operation. If true, the order was successfully recalled.
-   **[responseMessages](atlas.en-us.packagingGuide.meta/packagingGuide/apex_COA_ServiceOrderCloneOutput_responseMessages.htm)**

-   **[originalServiceOrderId](atlas.en-us.packagingGuide.meta/packagingGuide/apex_COA_ServiceOrderCloneOutput_originalServiceOrderId.htm)**
