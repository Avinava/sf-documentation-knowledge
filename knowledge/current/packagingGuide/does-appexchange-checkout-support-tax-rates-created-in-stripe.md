# Does AppExchange Checkout support tax rates created in Stripe?

> No. Although Stripe allows you to create tax rates, Checkout doesn’t support the
      Stripe rates. Salesforce internally manages tax rates, including rates for US sales tax and
      value-added tax (VAT).
