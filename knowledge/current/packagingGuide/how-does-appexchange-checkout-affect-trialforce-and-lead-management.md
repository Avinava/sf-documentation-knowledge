# How does AppExchange Checkout affect Trialforce and lead management?

> Checkout doesn’t affect your Trialforce configuration or how you manage leads. However,
  when a customer signs up for a trial using Checkout, the corresponding trial user is listed as
   Active in the License Management App (LMA).
