# Provide Test Drives on Your AppExchange Listing

To make test drives available on your AppExchange listing, go to the AppExchange Partner Console and connect your test drive org to the listing.


| User Permissions Needed |
| --- |
| To create or update AppExchange listings: | Manage Listings |

1.  Log in to the [Salesforce Partner Community](https://partners.salesforce.com/ "HTML (New Window)").
2.  Click **Publishing**.
3.  Click **Listings**, and then select a listing.
4.  Click **Grow Your Business**.
5.  In Test Drives, toggle on **Offer test drives**.
6.  Provide your test drive org ID and your evaluation user’s username and password (1).![The Partner Console Grow Your Business step with a highlight on Test Drives Org ID, Username, and Password](/docs/resources/img/en-us/260.0?doc_id=appexchange%2Fimages%2Fappexchange_test_drive.png&folder=packagingGuide)

View your live listing on AppExchange, and make sure that your test drive is functioning the way you expect it to.
