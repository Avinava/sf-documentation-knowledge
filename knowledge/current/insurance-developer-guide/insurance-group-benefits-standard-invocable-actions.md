# Insurance Group Benefits Standard Invocable Actions

Use the standard invocable actions available with Insurance Group Benefits to effectively manage the insurance plans offered to groups, such as employees and their dependents.

Available in: Lightning Experience

Available in: **Professional**, **Enterprise**, and **Unlimited** Editions of Digital Insurance with Digital Insurance Product Admin Add On, Digital Insurance Group Benefits AddOn, Digital Insurance Group Enrollment AddOn,Digital Insurance Group Benefits Customer Community AddOn, and Digital Insurance Group Benefits Partner Community AddOn licenses

-   **[Create Contacts and Users from Group Census Members Action](atlas.en-us.insurance_developer_guide.meta/insurance_developer_guide/actions_obj_create_contacts_and_users_from_group_census_members_or_group_census.htm)**
    Create contacts and portal users for group census or group census members.
-   **[Create Contract From Quote Action](atlas.en-us.insurance_developer_guide.meta/insurance_developer_guide/actions_obj_create_contract_from_quote.htm)**
    Create a group insurance contract from a quote.
-   **[Create Person Accounts and Users from Group Census Members Action](atlas.en-us.insurance_developer_guide.meta/insurance_developer_guide/actions_obj_create_person_accounts_and_users_from_group_census_members_or_group_census.htm)**
    Create person accounts and portal users for group census or group census members. This action supports bulk processing, custom field mapping, and duplicate detection.
-   **[Get Group Census Members Action](atlas.en-us.insurance_developer_guide.meta/insurance_developer_guide/actions_obj_get_group_census_members.htm)**
    Get the group census member records from the content document in the specified asynchronous bulk request item record.
-   **[Get Group Census Members and Plans Action](atlas.en-us.insurance_developer_guide.meta/insurance_developer_guide/actions_obj_get_group_census_members_and_plans.htm)**
    Get the group census member records and associated plans from the content document in the specified asynchronous bulk request item record.
-   **[Get Member Ratings and Contributions Action](atlas.en-us.insurance_developer_guide.meta/insurance_developer_guide/actions_obj_get_member_ratings_and_contributions.htm)**
    Get standard and prorated insurance rates for the specified group census members and their selected plan coverages, including contributions.
-   **[Process Member Enrollment Action](atlas.en-us.insurance_developer_guide.meta/insurance_developer_guide/actions_obj_process_member_enrollment.htm)**
    Create insurance policies for specified group census members as per their selected plans.
-   **[Save Group Census Members Data Action](atlas.en-us.insurance_developer_guide.meta/insurance_developer_guide/actions_obj_save_group_census_members_data.htm)**
    Create or update the group census member records for primary members, update the IDs of the dependent members, and create or update groups census member records for the dependent members.
-   **[Save Group Census Member Plans Data Action](atlas.en-us.insurance_developer_guide.meta/insurance_developer_guide/actions_obj_save_group_census_members_plans_data.htm)**
    Validate and save group census member plans for the new members. Update the group census member plans for existing members.
-   **[Validate Group Census Members Action](atlas.en-us.insurance_developer_guide.meta/insurance_developer_guide/actions_obj_validate_group_census_members.htm)**
    Validate the specified group census member records and assign default values to the specified fields.
-   **[Validate Group Census Members Plans Action](atlas.en-us.insurance_developer_guide.meta/insurance_developer_guide/actions_obj_validate_group_census_members_plans.htm)**
    Validate the specified group census member plan records and assign default values to the specified fields.
