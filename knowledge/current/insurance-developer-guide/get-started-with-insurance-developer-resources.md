# Get Started with Insurance Developer Resources

Use the developer resources of Insurance Cloud to manage end-to-end insurance processes. Create and configure quotes, manage policy transactions, and automate processes related to brokerage policies and commissions.

| Available in: Lightning Experience |
| --- |
| Available in: Enterprise, Unlimited, and Developer Editions |


Learn more about the developer resources that are available for these components.

## Insurance Brokerage

Manage insurance policy lifecycles. Automate processes related to Policy and Commissions Management. See [Insurance Brokerage](atlas.en-us.insurance_developer_guide.meta/insurance_developer_guide/insurance_brokerage_overview.htm "Use the Insurance Brokerages developer resources to automate processes related to Policy and Commissions Management. Invoke policy lifecycle services such as Renew, Repurpose, and Cancel using context service. Manage plan benefits using a flexible service layer and Product Catalog.").

## Insurance Claims Management

Manage insurance claims by automating, streamlining, and orchestrating the full lifecycle of a claim, from coverage validation to final settlement. See [Insurance Claims Management](atlas.en-us.insurance_developer_guide.meta/insurance_developer_guide/insurance_claim_overview.htm "Use the Insurance Claims developer resources to set up claim products and create dynamic first notice of loss intake flows for your users. Give claim adjusters a cohesive view of claims and financial data so they can efficiently assess and settle claims. Establish financial controls and automatically route requests for financial approval to the appropriate supervisors and managers.").

## Insurance Group Benefits

Manage insurance group benefit plans that re offered to and covering a group of people, such as employees and their dependents. See [Insurance Group Benefits](atlas.en-us.insurance_developer_guide.meta/insurance_developer_guide/insurance_group_benefits_overview.htm "Group benefits are insurance plans offered to and covering a group of people, such as employees and their dependents. Manage these insurance offerings in a rapidly evolving marketplace with Group Benefits on the Digital Insurance Platform.").

## Insurance Policy Administration

Manage policy lifecycles across quoting, issuance, endorsements, billing, and renewals. See [Insurance Policy Administration](atlas.en-us.insurance_developer_guide.meta/insurance_developer_guide/insurance_policy_overview.htm "Use the Insurance Policy developer resources to manage insurance policies comprehensively, for different transaction types, such as issuance, endorsement, renewal, and cancellation. Each transaction type creates an InsurancePolicyTransaction record.").

## Insurance Product Administration

Define the products you sell to customers, including their classifications, categories, and attributes. Create customized price adjustment methods and pricing procedures. Define rules that determine how your products behave when they're quoted, sold, and serviced. See [Insurance Product Administration](atlas.en-us.insurance_developer_guide.meta/insurance_developer_guide/insurance_product_administration_overview.htm "Use the Insurance Product Administration developer resources to define the products you sell to customers, including their classifications, categories, and attributes. Create customized price adjustment methods and pricing procedures. Define rules that determine how your products behave when they're quoted, sold, and serviced.").

## Insurance Quoting

Automate quoting experiences as part of a cohesive customer-centric experience. Create, update, configure, and delete quotes and quote line items. See [Insurance Quoting](atlas.en-us.insurance_developer_guide.meta/insurance_developer_guide/quote_overview.htm "Use the Insurance Quoting developer resources to create, update, configure, and delete quotes and quote line items.").
