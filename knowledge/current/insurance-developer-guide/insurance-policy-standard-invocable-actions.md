# Insurance Policy Standard Invocable Actions

Use the standard invocable actions available with Insurance Policy to create, manage, and endorse insurance policies.

-   **[Cancel Insurance Policy Action](atlas.en-us.insurance_developer_guide.meta/insurance_developer_guide/actions_obj_cancel_insurance_policy.htm)**
    Cancel an insurance policy by using a set of user inputs that represent policy details.
-   **[Create Out-of-sequence Policy Endorsement Action](atlas.en-us.insurance_developer_guide.meta/insurance_developer_guide/actions_obj_create_out_of_sequence_policy_endorsement.htm)**
    Create an out-of-sequence endorsement for an insurance policy.
-   **[Endorse Insurance Policy Action](atlas.en-us.insurance_developer_guide.meta/insurance_developer_guide/actions_obj_endorse_insurance_policy.htm)**
    Endorse an insurance policy by using a set of user inputs that represent policy details.
-   **[Endorse Multi-Root Insurance Policy Action](atlas.en-us.insurance_developer_guide.meta/insurance_developer_guide/actions_obj_endorse_multi_root_insurance_policy.htm)**
    Create an endorsement for an existing multi-root insurance policy to apply updates or changes to the policy and its associated child policies.
-   **[Get Insurance Policy Action](atlas.en-us.insurance_developer_guide.meta/insurance_developer_guide/actions_obj_get_insurance_policy.htm)**
    Get the details of an insurance policy.
-   **[Issue Insurance Policy Action](atlas.en-us.insurance_developer_guide.meta/insurance_developer_guide/actions_obj_issue_insurance_policy.htm)**
    Issue an insurance policy by using a set of user inputs that represent policy details.
-   **[Issue Multi-Root Insurance Policy Action](atlas.en-us.insurance_developer_guide.meta/insurance_developer_guide/actions_obj_issue_multi_root_insurance_policy.htm)**
    Create a new multi-root insurance policy along with its associated child policies asynchronously.
-   **[Reinstate Insurance Policy Action](atlas.en-us.insurance_developer_guide.meta/insurance_developer_guide/actions_obj_reinstate_insurance_policy.htm)**
    Reinstate a previously canceled insurance policy.
-   **[Renew Insurance Policy Action](atlas.en-us.insurance_developer_guide.meta/insurance_developer_guide/actions_obj_renew_insurance_policy.htm)**
    Renew an insurance policy by using a set of user inputs that represent policy details.
-   **[Renew Insurance Policies Action](atlas.en-us.insurance_developer_guide.meta/insurance_developer_guide/actions_obj_renew_insurance_policies.htm)**
    Process the renewal of insurance policies in bulk.
-   **[Renew Multi-Root Insurance Policy Action](atlas.en-us.insurance_developer_guide.meta/insurance_developer_guide/actions_obj_renew_multi_root_insurance_policy.htm)**
    Renew an existing multi-root insurance policy along with its associated child policies asynchronously.
