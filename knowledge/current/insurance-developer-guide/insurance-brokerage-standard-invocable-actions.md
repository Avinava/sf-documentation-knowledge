# Insurance Brokerage Standard Invocable Actions

Learn more about the standard invocable actions available with Insurance Brokerage.

-   **[Compute Producer Splits Action](atlas.en-us.insurance_developer_guide.meta/insurance_developer_guide/actions_obj_compute_producer_splits.htm)**
    Compute the producer splits for the producers associated with an Insurance Policy, for a Commission Statement Line Item.
-   **[Create Producer Commissions Action](atlas.en-us.insurance_developer_guide.meta/insurance_developer_guide/actions_obj_create_producer_commissions.htm)**
    Create records for the commissions that producers receive for the insurance policy associated with the specified commission statement line item, and update the commission statement line item record status.
-   **[Find Insurance Policy Action](atlas.en-us.insurance_developer_guide.meta/insurance_developer_guide/actions_obj_find_insurance_policy.htm)**
    Get the insurance policy associated with a commission statement line item that matches the specified criteria, and update the status of the commission statement line item record.
