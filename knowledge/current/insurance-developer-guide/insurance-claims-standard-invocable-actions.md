# Insurance Claims Standard Invocable Actions

Learn more about the standard invocable actions available with Insurance Claims.

-   **[Calculate Adjustments Action](atlas.en-us.insurance_developer_guide.meta/insurance_developer_guide/actions_obj_calculate_adjustments.htm)**
    Calculate the adjusted amount for a loss item and generate data for insurance policy limit tracking and claim coverage payment adjustment records.
-   **[Cancel Claim Coverage Payment Detail Action](atlas.en-us.insurance_developer_guide.meta/insurance_developer_guide/actions_obj_cancel_claim_coverage_payment_detail.htm)**
    Cancel payment for a claim coverage payment detail record.
-   **[Create Claim Action](atlas.en-us.insurance_developer_guide.meta/insurance_developer_guide/actions_obj_create_claim.htm)**
    Create a claim.
-   **[Create Claim Coverage Payment Detail Action](atlas.en-us.insurance_developer_guide.meta/insurance_developer_guide/actions_obj_create_claim_coverage_payment_detail.htm)**
    Create a claim coverage payment detail record.
-   **[Create Policy Limits Action](atlas.en-us.insurance_developer_guide.meta/insurance_developer_guide/actions_obj_create_policy_limits.htm)**
    Create insurance policy limit records for policy coverage and root attributes.
-   **[Delete Claim Coverage Payment Detail Action](atlas.en-us.insurance_developer_guide.meta/insurance_developer_guide/actions_obj_delete_claim_coverage_payment_detail.htm)**
    Remove unpaid claim coverage payment details by updating the status of the claim coverage payment detail record to Deleted.
-   **[Edit Claim Coverage Payment Detail Action](atlas.en-us.insurance_developer_guide.meta/insurance_developer_guide/actions_obj_edit_claim_cvr_payment_detail.htm)**
    Modify a claim coverage payment detail record.
-   **[Get Claim Action](atlas.en-us.insurance_developer_guide.meta/insurance_developer_guide/actions_obj_get_claim.htm)**
    Get the details of a claim.
-   **[Get Insurance Policy Current Standings Action](atlas.en-us.insurance_developer_guide.meta/insurance_developer_guide/actions_obj_get_insurance_policy_current_standings.htm)**
    Get the current standings of the insurance policy.
-   **[Open Claim Coverage Action](atlas.en-us.insurance_developer_guide.meta/insurance_developer_guide/actions_obj_open_claim_coverage.htm)**
    Open a claim coverage.
-   **[Pay Ex Gratia Claim Coverage Payment Detail Action](atlas.en-us.insurance_developer_guide.meta/insurance_developer_guide/actions_obj_pay_ex_gratia_claim_cvr_payment_detail.htm)**
    Process ex gratia payments for the claim coverage payment detail record when the adjusted amount is greater than the policy limit amount or count or both.
-   **[Pay Claim Coverage Payment Detail Action](atlas.en-us.insurance_developer_guide.meta/insurance_developer_guide/actions_obj_pay_claim_coverage_payment_detail.htm)**
    Initiate the payment for a claim coverage payment detail record, track the policy limit consumption for the payout, and update the status of the claim coverage payment detail record to Paid.
-   **[Recalculate Adjustments Action](atlas.en-us.insurance_developer_guide.meta/insurance_developer_guide/actions_obj_recalculate_adjustments.htm)**
    Recalculate the adjusted amount for a claim loss item to the maximum amount that’s allowed by the insurance policy.
-   **[Update Claim Action](atlas.en-us.insurance_developer_guide.meta/insurance_developer_guide/actions_obj_update_claim.htm)**
    Update a claim.
-   **[Update Claim Coverage Action](atlas.en-us.insurance_developer_guide.meta/insurance_developer_guide/actions_obj_update_claim_coverage.htm)**
    Update a claim coverage.
-   **[Verify Policy Coverage Action](atlas.en-us.insurance_developer_guide.meta/insurance_developer_guide/actions_obj_verify_policy_coverage.htm)**
    Verify whether a policy coverage is applicable for a specified loss date under an insurance policy, so that only eligible coverages are processed for claims.
