# Content Management

Use these resources to manage customer content.

-   **[Bulk Presentation (POST)](atlas.en-us.life_sciences_dev_guide.meta/life_sciences_dev_guide/connect_resources_bulk_presentation.htm)**
    Create a presentation and its associated content in bulk. You can upload presentation files as PDF or ZIP and associated metadata. The Health Cloud Starter and Life Sciences Commercial Admin Permission Sets are required for accessing this API. To know more, see [Email Template](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.lsc_email_get_ready.htm&language=en_US) and [Intelligent Content](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.lsc_intelligent_content_get_ready.htm&language=en_US).
-   **[Bulk Presentation (PATCH)](atlas.en-us.life_sciences_dev_guide.meta/life_sciences_dev_guide/connect_resources_bulk_presentation_patch.htm)**
    Update a presentation and its associated content in bulk. You can upload presentation files as PDF or ZIP and associating metadata. The Health Cloud Starter and Life Sciences Commercial Admin Permission Sets are required for accessing this API. To know more, see [Email Template](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.lsc_email_get_ready.htm&language=en_US) and [Intelligent Content](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.lsc_intelligent_content_get_ready.htm&language=en_US).
-   **[Email Template (POST)](atlas.en-us.life_sciences_dev_guide.meta/life_sciences_dev_guide/connect_resources_bulk_email_template.htm)**
    Create an email template. The Health Cloud Starter and Life Sciences Commercial Admin Permission Sets are required for accessing this API. To know more, see [Email Template](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.lsc_email_get_ready.htm&language=en_US) and [Intelligent Content](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.lsc_intelligent_content_get_ready.htm&language=en_US).
-   **[Email Template (PATCH)](atlas.en-us.life_sciences_dev_guide.meta/life_sciences_dev_guide/connect_resources_bulk_email_template_patch.htm)**
    Update an email template.The Health Cloud Starter and Life Sciences Commercial Admin Permission Sets are required for accessing this API. To know more, see [Email Template](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.lsc_email_get_ready.htm&language=en_US) and [Intelligent Content](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.lsc_intelligent_content_get_ready.htm&language=en_US).
-   **[Presentation Page Product (POST, PATCH)](atlas.en-us.life_sciences_dev_guide.meta/life_sciences_dev_guide/connect_resources_page_products.htm)**
    Create or update (delete) product and guidance associations for a presentation page. The Health Cloud Starter and Life Sciences Commercial Admin Permission Sets are required for accessing this API. To know more, see [Email Template](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.lsc_email_get_ready.htm&language=en_US) and [Intelligent Content](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.lsc_intelligent_content_get_ready.htm&language=en_US).
