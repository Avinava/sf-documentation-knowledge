# Trade Promotion Management Service APIs

Trade Promotion Management (TPM) is the process of planning, budgeting, presenting, and running incentive programs between consumer goods manufacturers and retailers.

| Available in: Lightning Experience in Enterprise and Unlimited Editions that have Consumer Goods Cloud Trade Promotion Management enabled. |
| --- |


For more information on using Trade Promotion Management, see [Set Up and Maintain Trade Promotion Management](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.tpm_getstart_parent.htm&type=5&language=en_US "HTML (New Window)").

-   **[Get Global Account Product List](atlas.en-us.retail_api.meta/retail_api/cgtpm_get_global_account_prod_list.htm)**
    Retrieves the account products from the global account product list based on the volumes and measures for the customer for the account.
-   **[Get Time-Dependent Account Product List](atlas.en-us.retail_api.meta/retail_api/cgtpm_get_time_dependent_account_prod_list.htm)**
    Retrieves an array of products from the time-dependent account product list.
-   **[Get Products](atlas.en-us.retail_api.meta/retail_api/cgtpm_get_products.htm)**
    Retrieve a list of products. The list is returned based on the Product Definition Level setting that you configured in the promotion template.
-   **[Get Report Data](atlas.en-us.retail_api.meta/retail_api/cgtpm_rtr_report_data.htm)**
    Get runtime report data for display on the user-interface.
-   **[Update Product Assortment](atlas.en-us.retail_api.meta/retail_api/cgtpm_update_product_assortment.htm)**
    Receives a list of products within the time-dependent account product list and updates it.
-   **[Update Promotion](atlas.en-us.retail_api.meta/retail_api/cgtpm_update_promotion.htm)**
    Updates the details of a given promotion in the promotion cache (without calculation).
