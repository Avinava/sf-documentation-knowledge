# Get Your Consumer Key

When you create an external client app, it generates a consumer key, also known as a client ID. This value allows Salesforce to identify your third-party app during headless identity flows. Learn how to get your consumer key so that you can use it when configuring headless flows.

| Available in: both Salesforce Classic (not available in all orgs) and Lightning Experience |
| --- |
| Available in: Enterprise, Unlimited, and Developer Editions |


1.  If you’re not on the external client app detail page from the previous step, go to your app page.
    1.  From Setup, in the Quick Find box, enter External, and then select **External Client App Manager**.
    2.  Find your app and click its name. On the app detail page, click the Settings tab.
2.  Expand the OAuth Settings section and click **Consumer Key and Secret**.

    Salesforce prompts you to verify your identity with one of your registered methods. After you verify your identity, you can see your consumer key.
