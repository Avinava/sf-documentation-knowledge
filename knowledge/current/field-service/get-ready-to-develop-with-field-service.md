# Get Ready to Develop with Field Service

To prepare to work programmatically with field service features, verify that Field Service is enabled in your org.

1.  From Setup, enter Field Service Settings in the Quick Find box, then select **Field Service Settings**.
2.  Verify that Field Service is enabled.
3.  Click **Save**.

You now have access to Salesforce’s standard field service objects, which are listed in this guide. However, that’s just the beginning! To get your feet wet before you start working programmatically with Field Service, it’s a good idea to walk through the setup tasks outlined in [Set Up Field Service](https://help.salesforce.com/articleView?id=fs_set_up.htm&language=en_US).
