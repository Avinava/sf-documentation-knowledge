# Resources

Here’s a list of Automotive Cloud Business API resources.

-   **[Inventory Visibility Product Transfer Action](atlas.en-us.automotive_cloud.meta/automotive_cloud/connect_resources_inventory_visibility_action.htm)**
    Returns the results for vehicle inventory transfer action search based on the inventory visibility actions configuration defined by the admin.
-   **[Orchestration Inbound Events (POST)](atlas.en-us.automotive_cloud.meta/automotive_cloud/connect_resources_inbound_events.htm)**
    Orchestrate and process inbound events from external sources.
-   **[Transformations](atlas.en-us.automotive_cloud.meta/automotive_cloud/auto_resources_transformations.htm)**
    Perform the business transformation of lead line item or lead preferred seller data (source object) to opportunities line item or opportunity preferred seller data (target object). The following are some of the transformation scenarios: LeadLineItem to OpportunityLineItem, and LeadPreferredSeller to OpportunityPreferredSeller.
