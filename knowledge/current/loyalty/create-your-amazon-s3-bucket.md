# Create Your Amazon S3 Bucket

Create a bucket to store your receipt images and any other data in AWS.

| Available in: all editions that have Loyalty Management enabled |
| --- |


1.  Log in to your AWS Management Console.
2.  From the Services menu, find and select **S3**.
3.  Click **Create bucket**.
4.  In AWS Region, select the region where you want the bucket to reside.
5.  Enter a name for your bucket.
6.  Click **Create bucket**.
