# Create an IAM Role

Create your Identity and Access Management (IAM) role to control the access to your Amazon S3 bucket. The IAM role ensures the security and functioning of AWS resources.

| Available in: all editions that have Loyalty Management enabled |
| --- |


1.  Log in to your AWS Management Console.
2.  From the Services menu, find and select **IAM**.
3.  From the navigation menu, click **Roles**.
4.  Click **Create role**.
5.  From the Service or use case dropdown list, select **API Gateway**, and then click **Next**.
6.  On the Add permissions page, click **Next**.
7.  Enter the role name and description, and then click **Create role**.
