# B2B Referral Management Standard Invocable Actions

Create and manage B2B referral programs for your organization using invocable actions.

-   **[Advocate Enrollment](atlas.en-us.referral_marketing.meta/referral_marketing/referral_actions_adv_enrollment.htm)**
    Enroll an existing or new customer as an advocate of a referral promotion.
-   **[Referral Event](atlas.en-us.referral_marketing.meta/referral_marketing/referral_actions_referral_event.htm)**
    Create referral event records when an advocate refers a friend, or when referred friends sign up or make a purchase.
