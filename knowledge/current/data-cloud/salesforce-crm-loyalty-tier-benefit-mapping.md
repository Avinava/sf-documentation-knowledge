# Salesforce CRM: Loyalty Tier Benefit Mapping

These data mappings are for the Loyalty Tier Benefit DLO found in the Loyalty Management bundle of the Salesforce CRM Connector for Data Cloud. A benefit that’s associated with a member’s tier in the loyalty program.

## Data Bundle

-   Loyalty Management

## DLO to DMO Mapping

These data mappings relate to the Loyalty Tier Benefit object included in the Salesforce CRM connector and how that data connects to DMOs. Learn more about the DLOs in the [Loyalty Management Developer Guide](https://developer.salesforce.com/docs/atlas.en-us.248.0.loyalty.meta/loyalty/loyalty_api_overview.htm).
