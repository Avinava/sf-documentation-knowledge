# Introduction to Grantmaking

Streamline the grant management process for both funders and applicants. Built on the Salesforce platform, Nonprofit Cloud for Grantmaking includes everything you need to give grants and manage your budgets.

| Available in: Lightning Experience |
| --- |
| Available in: Enterprise, Unlimited, and Developer Editions |


Use Grantmaking to:

-   Track organizations and people involved in grants and grant applications.
-   Create funding opportunities that include application details.
-   Manage reviews and approvals of grant applications and budgets.
-   When used with Experience Cloud, let grant seekers learn about and apply for funding opportunities, submit budgets and upload supporting documents.
