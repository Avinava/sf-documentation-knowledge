# Enable Appointment Distribution

Make resource selection for territories with relatively large numbers of resources easier by setting Appointment Distribution to show only a short list of least-consumed resources from your resource pool.

For information on enabling appointment distribution, see [Enable Appointment Distribution](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=platform.ls_enable_appointment_distribution.htm&type=5&language=en_US).
