# Responses

Learn more about the available response bodies of Stage Management Business APIs.

Refer to the [HTTP response code](https://developer.salesforce.com/docs/atlas.en-us.260.0.chatterapi.meta/chatterapi/connect_error_responses.htm "HTML (New Window)") to check whether the request was successful, as well as the error messages for the failed requests.

-   **[Fulfillment Step Details](atlas.en-us.industries_reference.meta/industries_reference/connect_responses_fulfillment_step_info.htm)**  
    Output representation of the fulfillment steps associated with a stage.
-   **[Stage Details](atlas.en-us.industries_reference.meta/industries_reference/connect_responses_object_stage_value_info.htm)**  
    Output representation of the details associated with a stage.
-   **[Stage Management Details](atlas.en-us.industries_reference.meta/industries_reference/connect_responses_record_stage_overview_output.htm)**  
    Output representation of the details of stage management.
