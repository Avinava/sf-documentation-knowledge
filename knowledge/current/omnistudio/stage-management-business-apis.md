# Stage Management Business APIs

# Stage Management Business APIs

Use the Stage Management Business APIs to get the list of stages and their associated fulfillment steps for a record.

## Available Resources

| Resource | Description |
| --- | --- |
| /connect/stage-management/kanban-view/recordId (GET) | Get the list of stages and their associated fulfillment steps for a specific record. |

-   **[REST Reference](atlas.en-us.industries_reference.meta/industries_reference/stage_management_apis_rest_references.htm)**  
    You can access Stage Management Business APIs by using the REST endpoint that follows similar conventions as Connect REST APIs.
