# Digital Lending Invocable Actions

# Digital Lending Invocable Actions

Use invocable actions supported with Digital Lending to create and manage contract documents.

-   **[Create Contract Document Generation Action](atlas.en-us.industries_reference.meta/industries_reference/actions_obj_create_contract_document_generation.htm)**  
    Create a contract for a specified record.
-   **[Check-In Contract Document Version Action](atlas.en-us.industries_reference.meta/industries_reference/actions_obj_check_in_contract_document_version.htm)**  
    Check-in a contract document version.
-   **[Perform Contract Action](atlas.en-us.industries_reference.meta/industries_reference/actions_obj_perform_contract.htm)**  
    Perform actions on a contract based on its status.
-   **[Send e-Sign Contract Action](atlas.en-us.industries_reference.meta/industries_reference/actions_obj_send_esign_contract.htm)**  
    Send a contract to specified recipients for e-signature.
