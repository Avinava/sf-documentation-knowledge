# Accept Inbound Calls in Omni-Channel

Now that you’ve added support for inbound calls, open the Voice Call Simulator and click New Inbound Call.

For details, see [Test Your Implementation with the Voice Call Simulator](atlas.en-us.voice_pt_developer_guide.meta/voice_pt_developer_guide/voice_pt_test_implementation.htm "The Voice Call Simulator component helps you get comfortable with Service Cloud Voice. As you set up Service Cloud Voice, use the simulator to walk through a variety of call scenarios.").

The call appears in the Omni-Channel utility. ![Omni-Channel utility window.](/docs/resources/img/en-us/260.0?doc_id=voice_pt%2Fimages%2Fomni_utility.png&folder=voice_pt_developer_guide)To accept the call, click the checkmark. You can mute, unmute, transfer, and end the call. Salesforce opens the VoiceCall record that is created. ![Omni-Channel utility and opened voice call record.](/docs/resources/img/en-us/260.0?doc_id=voice_pt%2Fimages%2Fvoice_call_record.png&folder=voice_pt_developer_guide)
