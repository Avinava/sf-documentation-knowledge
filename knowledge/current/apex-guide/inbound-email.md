# Inbound Email

Use Apex to work with email sent to Salesforce.

You can use Apex to receive and process email and attachments. The email is received by the Apex email service, and processed by Apex classes that utilize the InboundEmail object.

![Note](/docs/resources/img/en-us/260.0?doc_id=images%2Ficon_note.png&folder=apexcode)

#### Note

The Apex email service is only available in Developer, Enterprise, Unlimited, and Performance Edition organizations.

See [Apex Email Service](atlas.en-us.apexcode.meta/apexcode/apex_classes_email_inbound_what_is.htm "You can use email services to process the contents, headers, and attachments of inbound email. For example, you can create an email service that automatically creates contact records based on contact information in messages.").
