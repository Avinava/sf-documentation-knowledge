# sObject Collections

You can manage sObjects in lists, sets, and maps.

-   **[Lists of sObjects](atlas.en-us.apexcode.meta/apexcode/apex_list_sobject.htm)**
    Lists can contain sObjects among other types of elements. Lists of sObjects can be used for bulk processing of data.
-   **[Sorting Lists of sObjects](atlas.en-us.apexcode.meta/apexcode/apex_list_sorting_sobject.htm)**
    Using the List.sort method, you can sort lists of sObjects.
-   **[Expanding sObject and List Expressions](atlas.en-us.apexcode.meta/apexcode/langCon_apex_expressions_extending_SObjects.htm)**

-   **[Sets of Objects](atlas.en-us.apexcode.meta/apexcode/apex_set_sobject.htm)**
    Sets can contain sObjects among other types of elements.
-   **[Maps of sObjects](atlas.en-us.apexcode.meta/apexcode/apex_map_sobject.htm)**
    Map keys and values can be of any data type, including sObject types, such as Account.
